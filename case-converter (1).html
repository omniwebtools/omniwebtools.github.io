What is a Text Case Converter?

A text case converter is a simple online tool that changes how the letters in your text are capitalized. Instead of retyping everything by hand, you just paste your text and click one button — the tool does the rest in a split second.

This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your text is never uploaded or stored anywhere, so it stays completely private.

How to Use This Tool

Step 1. Paste or type your text into the box above.

Step 2. Click any case button — the text updates instantly so you can see the result right away.

Step 3. Click "Copy" to copy the converted text to your clipboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up and no limits on how much text you can convert.

Does it save my text anywhere?

No. Everything happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server or stored.

What's the difference between camelCase and PascalCase?

camelCase starts with a lowercase letter (thisIsCamelCase), while PascalCase starts with an uppercase letter (ThisIsPascalCase). Both remove spaces between words.
