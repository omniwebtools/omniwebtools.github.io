What is a Word Counter?

A word counter measures the length of your text in several ways — words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs — and estimates how long it would take an average person to read it out loud.

This is useful for writers checking essay or article length, students meeting assignment word limits, and social media users keeping posts within a character limit.

How Reading Time is Calculated

Reading time is estimated using an average adult reading speed of about 200 words per minute. This is just an estimate — actual reading speed varies depending on the reader and the complexity of the text.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool save my text?

No. Everything is calculated instantly in your browser. Your text is never sent anywhere.

How are sentences counted?

The tool counts groups of text that end in a period, question mark, or exclamation mark.

How are paragraphs counted?

A paragraph is counted as any block of text separated by a blank line.
