How This Tool Works

This converter uses your browser's built-in image processing to redraw your image and save it in a new format. Your image file never leaves your device — there is no upload to any server, which means it's completely private and works even with a slow internet connection.

JPG is best for photos and produces smaller file sizes. PNG supports transparency and is best for graphics, logos, and screenshots. WebP offers a good balance of small file size and quality, and is well supported by modern browsers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large images may take a moment longer to process since everything runs on your own device.

Will converting to JPG remove transparency?

Yes. JPG does not support transparent backgrounds, so any transparent areas will be filled with white when converting from PNG to JPG.

Does this tool reduce image quality?

JPG and WebP use a quality slider that affects file size versus sharpness. PNG conversion is lossless and keeps full quality.
